Research Labs

Synthesis Center

❮ ❯

This center focuses on how to structure environments around human interaction and psychology to fit needs for shelter, exploration, and socialization.Students imagine and create technology that enriches but does not complicate the lives of which it serves, creating new systems of interaction and boundaries of reality.This center cultivates collaboration, opportunities to meet like minded people interested in using computational methods to synthesize physical and digital feedback into a stimulating experience. Worlds are created in which navigation and expression is through physical gestures, sensations, and movements.


Acoustic Ecology


❮ ❯

This center examines ways to teach scientific concepts to the greater community with accessible costs, activities, and opportunities to get engaged and connect with the sharing of scientific knowledge. Through student involvement, science is explored through the lens of fabrication and creativity, revolutionizing and imagining solutions to current problems, and encouraging collaboration for higher effectiveness. Outreach is done through educational institutions, hobby practitioner spaces, and through the digital world. Collaboration occurs with students in art, engineering, critical design and creation, design research, and human computer interaction disciplines.

The Imaging Lyceum


❮ ❯

Founded on the idea of Aristotles Greek school, where students collaborated across various disciplines for research ambitions. The center supports transdisciplinary research of optics, imaging, and photography in both the virtual, computational, and physical sense. Students examine the ethics of these technologies, develop research methods, synthesize new technology experiences, conceptualize the future based on existing and hypothetical technologies, and think critically about education in the arts and STEM fields. Collaboration is led by art, engineering, science, and humanities students.




Meteor Studio


❮ ❯

The studio allows for students to build their skills in designing and fabricating augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), and visual computing in an interdisciplinary environment. Students learn and experiment with visual capture and processing systems for AR and VR, create immersive experiences involving the 5 senses in the hybrid visual/physical world, and design practical applications and uses for AR and VR systems.




Geometric Media Lab


❮ ❯

Founded in 2018 by Pavan Turaga and Max Bernstein Interdisciplinary collaboration occurs between health-science, mathematics and statistics, media arts, and machine learning and vision. Students analyze the fundamentals and composition of computational media, learn mathematical methodologies that work with the nature of computational media, and collaborate in an interdisciplinary environment to discover new algorithms and examine the uses for computational technology.




The SHaDe Lab


❮ ❯

This interdisciplinary lab is dedicated to studying climate in urban areas through three ways: identifying heat sensed through biometeorological instruments, experienced physiologically and physically by humans, and through simulations. Students can utilize MaRTy, a mobile biometeorological instrument with tools to collect weather data, MaRTiny, a smaller, more cost effective version of MaRTy with the added benefit of embedded computer vision, a Micrometeorological Bike
